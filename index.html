About

Drawn to light & detail

I'm Anna Alessandra — a photographer and artist based somewhere beautiful. My work explores the intersection of natural light, human emotion, and the textures that make a moment worth preserving.

Whether working in film or digital, portrait or landscape, I look for the warmth hiding in the ordinary.
